WHO ARE WE?

We are a creative agency specialising in event management, media strategies, video production and advertising.
We are currently based in Kuala Lumpur, Johor Bahru and Kuching.
We create memorable events, comprehensive media strategies, meaningful videos that empower and help our clients achieve their desired needs. What makes us distinctively different from other agencies is our unique management ‘team’. It comprises of specialists in their own fields. To us, ‘age is just a number’.
We move as one with the ultimate goal of giving our clients the best regardless of project size and scope.
